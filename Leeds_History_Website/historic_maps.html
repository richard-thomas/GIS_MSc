Other historic map resources and links

  • OpenStreetMap OOC (Out Of Copyright) - http://ooc.openstreetmap.org/ streamable - Including some historical (out of copyright) maps, particularly OS NPE 1:50k (1945-55): "New Popular Edition" http://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Out-of-copyright_maps
  • National Library Scotland OS NPE 50k Maps http://maps.nls.uk/os/6inch-england-and-wales/index.html - only streamable, but designed to be used in mashup
  • http://www.visionofbritain.org.uk/maps/?download=true - free download of original map sheets
  • http://www.ponies.me.uk/maps/osmap.html - a collection of old OS maps, viewable online with a variable-transparency google map overlay on top.
  • Ordnance Survey OpenData http://www.ordnancesurvey.co.uk/business-and-government/products/opendata-products.html - free (downloadable) data from OS - includes APIs for website/app map inclusion
  • Ordnance Survey OpenSpace API allows for access to high quality OS explorer mapping too: http://www.ordnancesurvey.co.uk/business-and-government/products/os-openspace/api/index.html - embedded streaming (only) for web-pages & mobile apps - can include high quality 1:50k rasters
  • http://www.tracksintime.wyjs.org.uk/ - free online access to the historic tithe maps which together span the modern Leeds Metropolitan District.
  • http://www.bl.uk/onlinegallery/onlineex/firemaps/england/yorkshire/mapsu145ubu14u1ufs001r.html - British Library Insurance Plan of Leeds (1902) - gives indication of land use

Academic Research only

Digimap (EDINA) http://digimap.edina.ac.uk/: a large collection of historic Ordnance Survey Maps are available:
  • Town plans at scales 1:500, 1:528 and 1:1056: 1st Edition (1848 - 1908), 2nd Edition (1864 - 1911), 3rd Edition (1893 - 1923), 4th Edition (1935 - 1939).
  • County Series (1:10560): 1st Edition (1849 - 1899), 1st Revision (1888 - 1914), 2nd Revision (1900 - 1949), 3rd Revision (1922 - 1969).
  • County Series (1:2500): 1st Edition (1854 - 1901), 1st Revision (1893 - 1915), 2nd Revision (1906 - 1939), 3rd Revision (1924 - 1949).
  • National Grid (1943-1996) at several scales: 1:1250 (1943-1993), 1:2500 (1943-1995), 1:10000 (1969-1996), 1:10560 (1948-1976)
However access is restricted to academics in higher education institutes for research and cannot be published on the web.
